everybody.

Every body and mind is naturally seeking happiness - to reduce stress and anxiety and find greater enjoyment in life. I’d love to share with you the mindful habits and practices that have helped me lead a happier and healthier life.

the practices.

Meditation and yoga are practices in which we allow our attention to rest in the present moment. We don’t need to be anything special and we don’t need to go anywhere to be a better ‘self’. With honest acceptance of what is in front of us and where we are in our life journey we allow ourselves to be.

what is happiness?

If you ask this question to one hundred people you would probably get a mix of answers. But my bet is that there would be one common theme - a connection, an investment of energy in whatever it is that is responsible for our pleasure. Whether it’s spending quality time with the family, lounging on the beach, or eating chocolate ice cream there is a commitment to that activity (the moment) in which we experience happiness.
